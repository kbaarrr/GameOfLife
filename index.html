Questions

Everything parents ask us

Do we own the camera kit?
No — and that's by design. The Curio Cam and HDMI kit remain Curio property, like the best membership hardware. That means we replace it free if it ever breaks, and you ship it back with a prepaid label within 30 days if you decide not to renew. If the kit isn't returned, a $250 device fee applies.
How do the daily sessions work?
Every day there are two live studio windows: Morning Studio (8:00am–12:00pm) and Afternoon Studio (2:00pm–6:00pm), in your local time zone. There's no booking — children pop in and out whenever they like. Each hour rotates to a new activity drawn from your chosen learning tracks.
Can we really choose what our child learns?
Yes. Curio is built around choice: select up to three learning tracks across languages, cultures, science, making, food and the arts — and swap them any time from your dashboard. A family in London can choose Arabic & Gulf Culture; a family in Riyadh can choose French Atelier. The world is the menu.
What does the first month free actually mean?
Exactly what it says. Sign up today, we ship your kit free, and you pay nothing for 30 days. Your annual (or 24-month) billing only begins after the free month — and if you cancel before it ends, you owe nothing; just return the kit.
What is the Wireless Bluetooth Kit?
An optional $100 one-time upgrade that makes the whole setup cable-free — the Curio Cam streams to your TV and speakers over Bluetooth and WiFi, no HDMI run across the living room. You can add it at signup or later from your dashboard.
Is it safe? Who are the teachers?
Every Curio educator is a vetted, background-checked early-years professional, and studios are moderated live. The camera streams only during sessions you join, with a visible recording light and a physical lens cover.
