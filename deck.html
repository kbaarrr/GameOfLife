Patient experience your satisfaction scores can feel.

Rihla brings facilitated immersive journeys to the wards where patients spend their longest, most anxious hours — a last skydive over the Palm, the Haram at dawn, the village they grew up in. A trained facilitator runs every session at the bedside; the family shares it on a second screen. Evidence-based, infection-control compliant, and designed to move the patient-satisfaction indicators your leadership is measured on.

The problem

Long hours, anxious patients, measured outcomes

  • Oncology, dialysis and paediatric patients sit through hundreds of repeat hours with little to look forward to.
  • Procedural anxiety drives distress, complaints and sedation use.
  • National programs now set explicit inpatient-satisfaction targets — and hospitality investments alone have stopped differentiating.
The program

Experience care, run as a service

  • Facilitated immersive sessions on the ward — never hardware dumped on busy staff.
  • Headsets owned, sanitised and replaced by Rihla; single-use hygiene covers; protocols agreed with infection control.
  • Journey library incl. sacred journeys (offered with religious guidance) and bespoke patient commissions.
  • Quarterly reporting mapped to your satisfaction KPIs.
